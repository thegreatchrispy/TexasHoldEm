The Rules Are Simple.

Get all of your opponent's chips. The two ways of accomplishing this goal are to have the better hand, or get the opponents to fold. 
Does that sound easy? Well generations of players and stakeholders have taken part in immense tournaments played based off of such simple rules. 
So let's see why this game can be a beautiful dance of bluff and confidence that can end equally in enormous losses, or immaculate wins.
